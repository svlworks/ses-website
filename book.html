THE DEFINITIVE GUIDE

SELLEBRITY 2.0

The definitive playbook for attracting, serving, and keeping high-profile clients.

To build a world-class practice servicing professional athletes, entertainers, and prominent public figures, standard professional advice doesn't cut it. These clients operate in highly volatile environments, demand absolute discretion, and face complex legal, financial, and promotional realities.

Written by the founders and council members of the Sports & Entertainment Society, SELLEBRITY 2.0 serves as the official curriculum for the Certified Sports & Entertainment Professional (CSEP) designation. This book lays out the exact frameworks, compliance protocols, and strategic partnership tactics needed to elevate your service standards to the Sellebrity standard.
