CERTIFICATION ROADMAP

Steps to Earning CSEP

A rigorous four-stage pipeline to earn your credential and enter the Sellebrity directory.

I

Submit Experience & Vetting

Submit your professional credentials, licenses, and references. Applicants must demonstrate a clean compliance history and show active operations in wealth management, law, real estate, or lifestyle services.

II

Master Curriculum & Coursework

Study the official SELLEBRITY 2.0 syllabus. You will undergo 36 hours of rigorous training modules spanning asset protection, crisis management, non-disclosure protocols, and high-stakes marketing dynamics.

III

Comprehensive Certification Exam

Pass the CSEP Examination, a proctored, 150-question examination assessing your practical knowledge, compliance expertise, and ethical standing under the Sellebrity Code.

IV

Council Inductions & Referral Registry

Receive formal endorsement from the Sellebrity Council. Unlock your verified membership registry profile and receive immediate access to the elite referral network of CSEP professionals.
