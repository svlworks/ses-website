The Sports & Entertainment Society is a premier membership network for professionals who specialize in servicing celebrities, professional athletes, and entertainers. Our members span every industry that touches the elite — wealth management, business management, law, real estate, technology, luxury brands, travel, health and wellness, talent representation, and beyond. Membership connects you to the most trained, vetted, and influential service providers in the sports and entertainment world, opening doors to referrals, strategic partnerships, exclusive networking, and ongoing education in this fast-moving niche.

SES also serves as the credentialing home for the Certified Sports & Entertainment Professional (CSEP) designation to become a “Sellebrity,” one who serves professional athletes, entertainers, or public figures at the highest standards of excellence, discretion, and expertise. This is the definitive standard for professionals serious about attracting, serving, and keeping high-profile clients. The CSEP designation is quickly becoming the most recognized mark of excellence in the sports and entertainment service industry. Whether you are an established specialist or an ambitious professional ready to break into this space, the Sports & Entertainment Society gives you the tools, the network, and the credibility to rise to the top.

SELLEBRITY /ˈseləˌbritē/ noun

A professional who serves professional athletes, entertainers, or public figures at the highest standards of excellence, discretion, and expertise.

  • plural: Sellebrities.
  • adjective: Sellebrity (as in Sellebrity standard, Sellebrity Certified).

— derived from sell (to offer one's professional services) + celebrity (a person of significant public prominence).
